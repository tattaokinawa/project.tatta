How it works ;

TATTA is simple solution for beach clean up activity.

Player;

1,You go to our partner and buy TATTA bag. And Return it to the same place after beach clean.

2,You go to TATTA page and find sponsors and apply TATTA bag and use it.

Sponsor:

You become somebody’s TATTA bag sponsor.

You buy TATTA online and post to SNS.

You approve when Player’s offer using your TATTA bag.

 

Where to get TATTA bags

TATTA bags can be obtained from Family Mart convienience stores on Ishigaki island.
